四级作文模板

1.问题解决:

题目：The importance of speaking ability and how to improve it.

第一段：提出问题

==With the rapid (development/communication/globalization) of (economy/culture/society/technology/medical service/education)==, it is of great necessity for yougsters to improve our speaking ability.

==The following solutions can account for this phenomenon/issue.==

第二段：论点+论据

==First and foremost,there is no doubt that== we should practice it more.

==Based on a most recent servay, it is revealed that majority of successful social elites admitted that== they prefer practicing new things (oral English)

==Furthermore,it is obvious that== it is benefial for students to make foreign friends. ==Where there are friends, there is communications==. (万能句型)Therefore,our speaking ability can be improved.

==Last but not least, no one can deny that it is high time that== we can travel to an English speaking country.==Only in this way,we can== （倒装句）experience language culture ourselves.(have a better life)

第三段：重申观点

==In a word,it is clear that== we should promote our speaking ability. If we try our utmost to develop our spoken English, the future of our life will be both promingsing and rosy.

2.观点选择：

option choice choose decision

题目：

Suppose you have two options upon graduation: one is to find a job somewhere other to start a business of your own.

三个段落：

1段：阐述问题，列出不同观点，做出选择

==With the rapid (development/communication/globalization) of (economy/culture/society/technology/medical service/education)==, nowadays youngster/students ==have different options upon== graduation.Some think that to find a job somewhere is beneficial, while others thinks to start a business of your own is a better chioce. ==Personally, I prefer the former/latter.The following reasons can account for my preference.==

2段：论点+论据

==First and foremost, there is no doubt that== to find a job somewhere is more free. ==Based on a most recent servay, it is revealed that majority of successful social elites admitted that== their careers are free.

==Additionally,it is obviously that== we can make useful friends when we have a decent job.==Where there are work places,there are== friends（where there is a will,there is a way句型套）.
==Last but not least,no one can deny that== to find a job is easier than to start a company.==Though==（来个转折） some people do succeed in starting a business of their own, most of entrepreneurs failed when they just start.

结尾段：重申观点+提出希望

==In a word,A is better /more suitable than B.== ==If we spare no efforts to work hard==（如果我们不遗余力地做某事） in our career,the future of our life will be promising and rosy.

3.现象解释型

题目：the challenges of living in a big city.

1段：阐述背景+提出观点+过渡句

==With the rapid development of society/economy/education/culture/medical service==,there is an increasing number of citizens moving from suburbs to urban area.(阐述背景)There are a large number of challenges of living in a big cities.（提出观点）The followings are some of them.（过渡句）

2段：3论点+2论据

==First and foremost,there is no doubt that== the cost of housing will be a huge problem. ==Based on a most recent survey, a majority of successful social elites admitted that== they have a huge expencse on housing.

==Furthermore, it is obvious that== the competition is stronger in big cities.

Where there are people, there is competition.(万能句型) There are more people in big cities.

==Last but not least, needless to say that== the traffic condition in big cities is worse than in rural area. Athough the roads are wider in big cities, there are more vhicles.

3段：重申观点+提出希望

In a word, it is of great necessity for the residents to face the challenges. If ==we spare no efforts to== promote ourselves,the future of our life will be promosing and rosy.

分析问题：

​ Digging into it(深挖其本质),indeed we need to consider it seriously.____________(问题)The following reasons can accout for this issue.(过渡句)

​ First and foremost,________

​ Furthermore,_________

5.新闻

题目：write a news report to your campus newspaper on a visit to a local farm organized by your Student Union.

新闻文体：客观（第三人称）

​ 过去式（表示事件的结果）+过去进行时（事件的背景）

新闻的结构：（先出结果+再出细节+意义）

三个段落：

  1. 时间，地点，参与者，事件本身，事件结果
  2. 三个具体事件+三句补足
  3. 新闻的意义

On December First,Monday,the student Union organized an meaningful activity to visit the local farm, in which 300 volunteers actively participated. It turns out to be a great success.

This activity ==was aimed at== cultivating the ability of practical ability of students ==by== visiting the farmland and helping the peasants with hard labours. ==In the early morning== just after the arrival of students,the students watered the crops in the field for farmers.(事件1+)When asked about them responded with a smile,saying “what a wonderful practice and I really appreciate this experience,for it makes me learn to care more for others in need/use my knowledge in practice.”（万能补足语1）

==After the lunch==, the farmers held an experience sharing meeting,in which the students anwsered various useful questions.(事件2+补足语2)Some students showed how to use technology to improve the productivity.

==In the evening==,the students and the farmers enjoyed a feast together,which was prepared by the students. （事件3+补足语3）The peasants prasied the cooking of students.

意义：

This activity turns out to be a beneficial event for both sides. The Student Union expressed that there will be more meaningful events in the future./More efforts will be made to help farmers in the rural areas.

8.通知

模板：

第一段：描述通知

  • 第一句：the notice is for the purpose of_________(doing sth)通知目的

  • 第二句：the forthcoming event is aimed at_________(doing sth)

    eg：the forthcoming event is amied at improving the students’ knowledge to pass the CET4 in the next month.活动的目的在于提高学生知识，以通过下月将要到来的英语四级

  • 第三句：The followings are the details.

第二段：按要求造句

一般为:时间，地点，活动内容，注意事项

万能例句：

  • 时间：Every participant involved must attend the event on time at 7:00 pm.所有参加者必须准时在晚上7点到场。

  • 地点：The duration（时长） of this activity is almost 2 hours.So the auditorium is chosen as the site.Please bring your ids to be checked in the front door.

  • 活动内容：Last but not least,there will be some intersting agendas,which you must pay attention to. For example,votes and Q&A.

    最后但不重要的是，会有一些有趣的议程，你必须关注。例如，投票和问答。

第三段：呼吁/答疑+联系方式

​ if you have any questions,you are free to contact at 192974924 in the working hours.
